HOW TO RECOGNISE WILD ANIMALS

Asian Lion

  • A large and tawny beast whose roar is enough to kill you.

Bengal Tiger

  • If a noble wild beast eats you, then it is the Bengal tiger

Leopard

  • If an animal only leaps on you again and again.

Bear

  • A creature whose hug is enough to kill you

Hyena and Crocodile

  • Hyenas come with merry smiles, but if they weep they are crocodiles.

Chameleon

  • If there is nothing on the tree, know that it's a chameleon
